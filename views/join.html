Rules

NeXs basics (minimal, but serious)

If you match these, you’ll thrive here.

Show up & ship

Small weekly progress beats perfect plans. Bring something: a commit, a sketch, a test, a demo.

Be kind, be sharp

We critique the work, not the person. Ask great questions. Help others unblock.

Learn in public

Share notes, prototypes, and failures. Credit collaborators. No plagiarism.

Own your time

If you can’t make it, tell the team early. We respect classes—just be consistent.

Guidelines How we work
  • Weekly check-in + goal for the week
  • Use a single place for updates (WhatsApp/Discord + GitHub)
  • Build small, merge often, document lightly
  • Demo day culture: show what’s real
Conduct Respect & safety
  • No harassment, hate, or bullying
  • Respect privacy: don’t share personal data without permission
  • Ask before recording photos/videos in sessions
  • If something feels wrong, contact the coordinators
Selection What we look for
  • Consistency > certificates
  • Curiosity and learning attitude
  • Team mindset and communication
  • Clear interest in a domain (or willingness to explore)
