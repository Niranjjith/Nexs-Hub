About NeXs

NeXs (Next eXperience Space)

A student-driven innovation community bridging academic learning with real-world technology development.

Overview

NeXs was established as part of the Mini Tech Park initiative to provide a structured, collaborative environment where students can explore ideas, build projects, and gain practical industry-aligned experience.

Unlike classroom-only learning, NeXs emphasizes hands-on development, teamwork, and applied problem-solving. Students contribute to real projects, participate in skill-building sessions, and work with emerging technologies in a delivery-focused setting.
